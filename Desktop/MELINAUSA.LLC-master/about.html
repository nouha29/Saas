Who We Are

At Melina USA LLC, we specialize in the design, supply, and expert installation of premium wood-based furniture and interior elements. Located in Boca Raton, Florida, we offer a curated selection of handcrafted wood products including TV units, dining tables, bookshelves, consoles, study desks, chairs, coffee tables, makeup tables, and multi-functional cabinets.

Beyond retail, we provide professional installation services for woodwork used in interior design and renovation projects—bringing craftsmanship and precision to every detail.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality. From seamless joints and smooth finishes to designs that enhance the character of a space, every product is built to last. Our work blends timeless aesthetics with practical functionality—creating pieces that form the backdrop of life’s most meaningful moments.

In addition, we offer finishing carpentry services that add sophistication and style to any environment. Whether for residential or commercial spaces, we deliver a refined, architectural finish that transforms interiors from ordinary to exceptional.

Our dedicated customer service team is here to guide you—offering expert knowledge, product insight, and personalized support throughout your experience. At Melina USA LLC, we’re passionate about delivering exceptional results through quality craftsmanship, reliable service, and genuine care—every step of the way.
